Food name

45 min 6 portions

meatballs:

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 tbs bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • salt
  • pepper

tomato sauce:

  • 1 celery
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 200 ml water
  • 50 ml cream
  • salt
  • pepper

Description:

These meatballs are simmered in the tomato sauce to make them as tasty and juicy as possible.

Serve these with a sidedish of your choise. my favorites are pasta or potato wedges. To serve sprinkle with freshly grated parmesan.

Instructions:

For the meatballs:

  • Chop the onion and saute it in the butter until slightly browned and let cool.
  • Crack the egg in to bowl, whisk lightly and add breadcrumbs. Mix together and let sit for a while
  • Add the beef, sauted onion and the spices to the bowl and combine all ingredients.
  • Roll the mixture into a golfball sized meatballs and set a side.

For the sauce:

  • Cut the onion, carrot and celery to small cubes.
  • Melt the butter in a pan and add the cut vegetables, saute for ~10 minutes or until softened.
  • Add tomato paste and spices, and let cook for a while.
  • Deglaze the pan with red wine, once the wine is mostly cooked add the chopped tomatoes. Rinse the tomato tin with the water and add to pan.
  • Add the prerolled meatballs to the sauce and cover with a lid, let cook for 30 minutes. Stir the meatballs once in a while.
  • Add the cream and ripped basil leaves and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Serve with sidedish of choise and freshly grated parmesan.
